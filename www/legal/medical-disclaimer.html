1. Not a substitute for professional care

Workout plans, nutrition targets, body-composition estimates, calculators (BMI, BMR, TDEE, and similar), Health Connect data, and any AI-generated insight in this app are for general informational and self-tracking purposes only. None of it is a substitute for advice from a doctor, dietitian, physical therapist, or other qualified professional who knows your specific medical history.

2. Estimates, not diagnoses

Figures like body fat %, BMI, calorie targets, heart-rate zones, and similar values are calculated from standard formulas and the data you provide (or your device syncs) — they are estimates, not clinical measurements, and can be meaningfully wrong for a given individual (e.g. athletes, older adults, certain body types). Do not use them to diagnose, rule out, or manage any medical condition.

3. Exercise carries real risk

Physical exercise, including anything logged, suggested, or timed in this app (workouts, HYROX training, hold/rest timers, routines), carries a risk of injury. Stop immediately and seek medical attention if you feel pain, dizziness, chest discomfort, or shortness of breath during any activity. Use proper form, appropriate equipment, and your own judgment — the app cannot see you and cannot correct your technique.

4. Third-party and imported data

Data synced from Health Connect, uploaded medical reports, or imported files reflect whatever the source device, lab, or document says. IGNYT does not verify their medical accuracy and is not responsible for errors in data it did not generate.

5. Future AI features

Any current or future AI-assisted feature in this app (health insights, suggestions, coaching) is designed to never provide a diagnosis and will always recommend consulting a qualified healthcare professional for medical concerns. Treat AI output the same as any other estimate in this app: informational, not clinical.

6. In an emergency

If you believe you are having a medical emergency, contact your local emergency services immediately. Do not rely on this app, or wait to check it, in an emergency.
